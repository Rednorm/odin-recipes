Hamburger

Description

Beef patty sandwiched between two big buns with sesame seeds. It is complimented with mayo sauce, lettuce and onions, seasoned with salt,pepper and sugar.

Ingredients

  • 2x Buns
  • 1x Beef Patty
  • Onions
  • Mayo

Steps

  1. Cook the patty
  2. Cook the buns
  3. Put patty on one of the buns
  4. Add mayo sauce and onions
  5. Serve
